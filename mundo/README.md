## TL;DR (mid-game-glance summary)

**Standard build:** Doran's Shield → Warmog's → Heartsteel → Boots of Swiftness → Titanic Hydra → Dead Man's Plate → Jak'Sho

**Runes:** Resolve (Grasp / Demolish / Second Wind / Overgrowth) + Inspiration (Magical Footwear / Approach Velocity)

**Spells:** Ghost + Ignite

**Skill priority:** R > Q > W > E (most matchups; some E-max — see matchups.md)

**The two rules:**
1. Cleaver discipline — missed Q at rank 1 costs ~50 HP, hit a champ refunds full cost
2. W *right before* enemy burst, not at the start — first 0.25s stores almost all damage as grey HP

**If you don't know what to do, group.**
