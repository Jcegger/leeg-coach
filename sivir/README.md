## TL;DR (mid-game-glance summary)

**Standard build:** Doran's Blade → Yun Tal Wildarrows → Berserker's → Infinity Edge → Lord Dominik's → Stormrazor → Guardian Angel → Bloodthirster

**Runes:** Precision (Lethal Tempo / Presence of Mind / Bloodline / Cut Down) + Sorcery (Manaflow Band / Gathering Storm)

**Spells:** Barrier + Flash

**Skill priority:** Q > W > E (R at 6/11/16) — guide author goes E 2nd, W 3rd

**Bans:** Vayne, Twitch (author's go-to is Vayne)

**The two rules:**
1. **Spell Shield is sacred.** One charge, 22-second CD. Save it for engage CC (hooks, Leona E, Morg Q) or fight-deciding ults — not minor pokes.
2. **You scale, you don't bully.** Don't trade pre-Yun Tal. Farm with W, push waves, get to items.

**If you don't know what to do, shove and rotate.** No one waveclears like Sivir.
