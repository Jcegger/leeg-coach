# Sivir — Playbook

**Source provenance.** This playbook is **not from the Mobafire guide** — heeiseenbeerg's guide is build-only and doesn't include playbook prose. The notes below are general Sivir/ADC knowledge written for this repo. Adjust as you learn the champion.

## Pros / Cons

**Pros**
- Best waveclear of any ADC — W (Ricochet) shoves entire waves in one rotation
- Shield (E) eats targeted CC — defines the matchup against hook supports
- Global team MS ult (R) — strongest engage/disengage tool in any ADC kit
- Hard scaling: weak early, monster mid-late with full crit
- Hard to gank with R + Spell Shield in your pocket
- Excellent at sieging towers and rotating around the map
- Cheap mana costs once Manaflow stacks

**Cons**
- Auto range is only 500 — outranged by Caitlyn, Tristana late, Kog'Maw, Senna, Aphelios Calibrum
- No mobility — no dash, no jump. Flash + R is your only escape
- Weak in early-game 1v1 trades; needs items to come online
- No personal CC — can't lock anyone down
- Spell Shield is a single-charge cooldown; if you waste it, the next CC kills you
- Q is mana-hungry pre-Manaflow Band

## Ability cheat-sheet

### Passive — Fleet of Foot
- Hitting an enemy champion with an auto-attack grants bonus MS for ~2s
- Use it to stick to kited targets in trades, or to escape after a poke

### Q — Boomerang Blade
- 1100-range skillshot, returns to her — can hit twice (only second hit on the same target deals reduced damage)
- Procs Manaflow Band stacks
- Main poke tool — hold mid-trade for the slow-then-shred combo

### W — Ricochet
- Toggle: next 4 (max) autos bounce to nearby enemies
- Each bounce deals reduced damage, but procs on-hit (Yun Tal stacks too)
- **The defining waveclear ability** — one rotation clears a full wave with IE/Yun Tal
- Activates on cast — counts as an auto reset

### E — Spell Shield
- Blocks the next enemy ability (single target or AoE on you, projectile or instant)
- Restores mana on successful block
- **Defining ability** — eats Blitz hooks, Morg Q's, Pyke hooks, Naut Q's, ult-cast burst, etc.
- 22-second base cooldown — be deliberate about when you commit it

### R — On The Hunt
- Self + global team move speed for ~10s, plus self attack speed
- Engage tool: lets your team commit to a fight from far away
- Disengage tool: lets your team peel off a bad fight or chase down a kill
- Mana-free; use it often, don't hoard for the perfect fight

## Lane phase (levels 1–6)

- **You're not a lane bully.** Farm safely, don't trade unless you have a clear advantage.
- **Shove or freeze deliberately.** Sivir's W shoves harder than almost anyone — use that to set up dives, recalls, or roams from your support.
- **Mana management:** without Manaflow Band stacked, Q is expensive. Use it to last-hit cannons or to harass when you'll win the trade.
- **Spell Shield discipline:** don't reflexively pop it on minor pokes. Save it for engages (hooks, Leona E, Naut hook, Morg Q). A blown shield often means a death.
- **Use the bushes:** standing in tribush forces enemies to either skill-shot blind (you can shield) or walk in (you can W to push them out).
- **Ricochet (W) in trades:** activate W before an auto trade to multiply damage, especially when minions are nearby. Hard counter to enemies who hide behind their wave.
- **Don't over-push without vision.** Sivir has no escape — once shoved, you're gankable. Drop a control ward in tribush before each shove.

## Mid-game (post-1st item, ~10–20 min)

- **Yun Tal online = power spike.** You should look to fight or take an objective right after.
- **Push side waves into towers, rotate.** Sivir is the best ADC for shoving lanes and joining mid for grubs / drag.
- **R for rotations:** use ult to get to objectives faster than the enemy, or to engage on a caught-out enemy.
- **Don't 1v1.** Even with items, Sivir loses solo fights to Vayne, Twitch, Kai'Sa, Samira, Zeri. Always fight with your team.
- **Tower sieging:** with a frontline, you can shove and chunk towers fast — W procs on towers and your range lets you sit safely behind minions.

## Late-game / teamfighting

- **Position behind your frontline.** You have no mobility — if you get jumped, you die. Spell Shield is one charge.
- **R on engage or disengage** — proactive use is fine, you'll have it back.
- **Spell Shield priority targets:**
  - Long-channel kills (Malzahar R, Warwick R, Skarner R)
  - Engage hooks (Blitz, Naut, Thresh, Pyke)
  - Burst combos (Morgana Q, Leona E, Zoe E, Ahri E)
  - Ults that change a fight (Sona R, Karthus R, Cassio R)
- **W in teamfights:** activate W on engage, hit the closest target, let it bounce. Yun Tal stacks fast.
- **Kite back with passive MS:** auto → step → auto. Don't stand still.
- **Splitpush:** Sivir can sidelane safely with R disengage. Push with W, threaten with Q from range, R out if they collapse.

## Common combos

- **Trade poke:** W active → Q (auto-shielded by W bounces) → auto reset on W activation = ~1.5x damage burst
- **All-in (with engage support):** R (for MS) → W (toggle) → engage → Q on lowest HP target → autos
- **Escape:** R (MS) → Flash if needed → E to shield the chase ability

## Win conditions

- **Get to mid-game even.** You don't need to win lane; you need to not die.
- **Group at 2 items.** Sivir is at her best as the centerpiece of a coordinated mid-game push.
- **Hit objectives on cooldown.** Your waveclear lets you be the lane that pressures sides while your team takes drag/baron.
- **Don't fight 1v1.** Always force 2v2+, ideally 5v5 with R engage.

## Pairing notes (supports)

**Best with you:**
- **Engage tanks** (Leona, Nautilus, Rell, Rakan): they CC, you W-burst onto the locked target.
- **Enchanters** (Lulu, Janna, Milio, Soraka): you scale, they peel — late-game monster.
- **Threat supports** (Pyke, Pantheon): create kill pressure when you can't.

**Less synergistic (still playable):**
- **Yuumi** — she's late-scaling like you, but lane is rough since she does no damage and Sivir can't engage.
- **Brand / Zyra mage supports** — you don't need their poke (you have Q), and their lack of peel hurts you.
