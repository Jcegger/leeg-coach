# Sivir — Matchups

**Source provenance.** Only the **Extreme** entries (Vayne, Twitch) come from [heeiseenbeerg's guide](https://www.mobafire.com/league-of-legends/build/16-08-the-ultimate-sivir-build-648305) — the author left the rest of the threats section blank. Everything else is general Sivir/ADC knowledge written for this repo; treat tier classifications as a starting point and adjust as you learn the lane.

Bot lane is two enemies, so this file has both **ADC** and **support** entries. The CLI shows whichever enemy champion matches.

Threat tiers (worst → best for you): **Extreme** > **Major** > **Even** > **Minor** > **Tiny**

## Quick index

**Extreme (2):** Vayne, Twitch

**Major — ADCs (5):** Draven, Caitlyn, Lucian, Samira, Tristana
**Major — Supports (4):** Leona, Pyke, Zyra, Brand

**Even — ADCs (12):** Aphelios, Ashe, Ezreal, Jhin, Jinx, Kai Sa, Kalista, Kog Maw, Nilah, Varus, Xayah, Zeri
**Even — Supports (8):** Thresh, Nautilus, Blitzcrank, Rakan, Rell, Lulu, Karma, Bard

**Minor — ADCs (4):** Miss Fortune, Senna (also playable sup), Smolder, Corki
**Minor — Supports (8):** Soraka, Sona, Janna, Nami, Milio, Yuumi, Renata Glasc, Morgana

**Tiny — Supports (5):** Tahm Kench, Taric, Alistar, Braum, Zilean

## Extreme threats

### Vayne

Vayne is very hard to play against as Sivir, and due to her high presence, she is currently my go-to ban.

### Twitch

Twitch is similar to Vayne in terms of matchup difficulty but is slightly less popular, which is why I usually ban Vayne over him. He is another ban option if you hate playing against him.

## Major threats

### Draven

Lane bully who outdamages you in any extended trade pre-6. Don't trade autos with him while his axes are stacking — wait for him to drop a passive or use Stand Aside. Spell Shield (E) eats his axe-toss W rarely matters; the real danger is his auto damage. Stay behind minions, farm with W when you can't get autos in safely. He fells off relative to you by 2 items — if you survive lane down 0/2/0 even, you're winning the game.

### Caitlyn

Outranges you by 100 (650 → 550). Her traps and Q out-poke your Q, and she punishes hard if you step up to last-hit. Spell Shield her net (E). Don't try to win the early lane — farm under tower if you have to, scale, then outpush her in mid-game. Her teamfight is mediocre compared to yours.

### Lucian

Mobile lane bully. His Q double-procs his passive, so two Q's hurt; your shield blocks his Q. Time E to eat a Q if you can read the cooldown. Avoid staying in his W slow zone. Like Draven, he scales worse than you — don't die, push him out of XP range, scale.

### Samira

If she gets a level-3 or level-6 all-in on you, you die. Her W Spell Shield is very similar to yours but blocks projectiles instead of single-target abilities, so don't waste Q on her when W is up. Spell Shield (E) her ult, R, or her engage E. Pre-6 you outscale her trade with auto-shielded Q poke. Post-6 she has kill threat at any HP — respect it.

### Tristana

Outranges you at 18 (550 → 663). Her Rocket Jump (W) reset on kill is the threat. E-pop her Charge (E) to deny the bomb damage if you can. Pre-6 she's vulnerable; post-6 her R is a kill execute — don't stay in turret-dive range without Flash. Your R + W shoves her under tower — abuse that.

### Leona

Engage support with no projectile, so most of her CC isn't shieldable except her R (which is a small AoE stun). Her Zenith Blade (E) is the key ability — once she lands it she gets to you instantly. Stay further back than usual; if she E's onto you and your support doesn't peel, you're dead. R can be Spell Shielded.

### Pyke

His Q Bone Skewer is shieldable, his hook-stun is shieldable on the proc. Death from Below (R) executes — be careful at low HP. He roams a lot; ping mid when he leaves. Push him into tower so he can't roam. He's terrifying with execute resets in teamfights — don't stand next to a low ally.

### Zyra

Long-range poke with shieldable root (E). She zones you off CS hard. Her R has a long animation — Spell Shield it for huge value. Position behind plants/minions to break her seed traps. Her low mobility means you can punish overextensions.

### Brand

Pure poke threat. Spell Shield his Sear (W) stun on a hit target, or his root passive proc. His ult bounces — don't stack with allies in teamfights. Ignite or item antiheal isn't the answer; just don't get hit by W and you're fine.

## Even

### Aphelios

Threat depends on his guns. Watch his offhand:
- Calibrum (red, sniper): outranges you, can mark + chunk. Stay back.
- Severum (purple, lifesteal): can sustain through poke, weak range.
- Gravitum (cyan, root): root is shieldable.
- Infernum (orange, AoE): waveclear war — you win this.
- Crescendum (white, chakram): close range, you win.
**Calibrum + Gravitum** is his strongest combo against you; **Severum + anything** is the easiest matchup.

### Ashe

Slow + poke. Volley (W) is a wave, not shieldable. Her R is shieldable — huge. She has zero mobility, so once you have R + items you run her down. Lane is a poke war; even.

### Ezreal

Mobile poke. Q is shieldable, W is not. His E + Flash is a kill threat. Lane is even — neither of you wants to all-in. Outshove him for mid-game pressure. His ult is shieldable for huge value if you read the cast.

### Jhin

Slow attack speed (4 shots, then reload) means he's vulnerable during reload. Trap-heavy zone control. His W root and R are both shieldable. Don't stand on his W mark when your support gets CC'd. Even lane; you outscale.

### Jinx

Hard scaling like you. Lane is a farm war. Her Q rocket form outranges your Q. Stay behind minions to avoid her Zap (W) — shieldable. Her chompers (E) are shieldable. Late game she has a slight DPS edge but you have R and waveclear.

### Kai Sa

Mid-late game threat with R reset potential. Her Q is a wave (not shieldable), W is single-target poke (shieldable). Dodge W and farm. Once she has 3 items she 1v1's you, so play with team. Push her into tower to deny her plasma stacking.

### Kalista

High range + dance trades + crazy mobility. Her bound support means engages without warning — track her support's positioning. Her E execution is your danger sign — don't let minions get to low HP near her. She falls off late; you outscale.

### Kog Maw

Late game monster. Q is single-target poke (shieldable), W is auto buff, E is wave + slow (not shieldable but wide). Pre-15 you outdamage him; post-15 with Navori + IE he chunks you faster than you chunk him. Push him out of lane, deny scaling.

### Nilah

Melee ADC with lifesteal sharing — she pumps her support's healing too. Her W parry is similar to your shield (blocks autos for a window). Her engage is W + Q hop. Stay back, kite, don't let her get on top of you. Her R is huge teamfight burst — don't group tightly.

### Varus

Poke or lethality build. His R is a chained root, shieldable on the initial target. Q charges; back off when he's channeling. His E is wave damage. Even lane; outscales you slightly with crit build, you outscale lethality.

### Xayah

Feather setup is the threat — when she has 3+ feathers behind you, expect E. Her R is invuln + knockup; not shieldable. She's mobile but squishy. Pre-6 you out-trade with W procs; post-6 respect her ult.

### Zeri

Bursty ADC with Q AS-scaling and high mobility from E (wall-jump). Her W is a long shieldable poke. Her R amps her damage hugely — Spell Shield the burst Q on cast if you can read it. Lane is even; she outscales slightly but you matchteamfight equally.

### Thresh

Hook (Q) is shieldable — eats his entire engage. Flay (E) is shieldable. Lantern is just utility. Track his hook cooldown; if it's up he can engage at any time. Easy to play against if you stay aware and use E wisely.

### Nautilus

Q hook is shieldable. His passive auto-root on first auto is not (it's an auto, not a spell). His R is shieldable. Stay outside Q range; he's slow. Once he engages without hook, fight back — you have R disengage.

### Blitzcrank

Hook (Q) is shieldable on hit (eats it entirely). Watch his stance — he's basically a one-trick. Stay behind minions. If he hooks anyway, you can't dodge it but you can E it on reaction if your reflexes are quick. Late game he's a teamfight peeler.

### Rakan

Engage support with W charm + Q heal poke. His charge (W) is shieldable — denies his entire engage. His R is wide AoE charm. Track him after he roams; he'll often link up with mid for ganks.

### Rell

Engage tank. Her W mount-up is the warning sign for engage. Her crash (W) is unshieldable AoE. Her ult pulls. She's slow without mount; pressure her positioning. In teamfights stay behind your frontline so she can't crash on you.

### Lulu

Peels her ADC hard. Her polymorph (W) is shieldable on you. Her ult on her ADC is what wins fights — focus the ADC before she ults if possible. She's not threatening directly but makes the lane slow.

### Karma

Poke support with shields. Her Q (especially R-Q) is shieldable. Her root (W) is shieldable. Sivir's W waveclear lets you push past her shield game. Even lane.

### Bard

Roaming meandering style. His Q (stun on second target) is shieldable on you, but it's a skill-shot pierced through minions. Track his roams — he's bot-lane present maybe 60% of the time. R is shieldable. Watch for his portal (E) into your lane.

## Minor threats

### Miss Fortune

Slow attack speed, no mobility. Her Q (Double Up) bounces — stay away from low minions. Her R is shieldable but you usually need to dodge. Lane is even early, you outscale and outwaveclear.

### Senna

Long-range poke that scales with souls — playable as ADC or support, treat the same. Her root (W) is shieldable. She has no mobility and weak waveclear. Push her into tower; deny her souls. You outscale her in pure DPS.

### Smolder

Stack-scaling ADC. His Q damage scales with stacks; deny CS. His E is a wave dash; he uses it for engage and escape. Push him out of lane to deny stacks. Pre-stack-25 he's weak; post-stack-125 he's a monster.

### Corki

Mage-style ADC. His Q is shieldable, R is shieldable (huge). His Valkyrie (W) is gap-close + escape. He's mana-hungry early; punish blue-to-blue swaps.

### Soraka

Pure heal-bot. Her Q+E silence/root combo is shieldable. Push her — she has no waveclear. Engage when her Astral Infusion (W) takes her below 30% HP.

### Sona

Aura buffs + heals. Her Q is poke, all of her abilities are short range. Push her under tower; she has no waveclear. Spell Shield her ult in teamfights — denies an entire engage.

### Janna

Pure peel. Her Q is shieldable (cancels the upward knockup). Her R is shieldable on a target ally (it's targeted on her ADC, not on you). Push her hard — she has no waveclear and can't trade.

### Nami

Bubble (Q) is shieldable, stun on second target. Her ult is a wave knockup. She has no mobility. Even lane, slight edge to you with W push.

### Milio

Pure enchanter. His ult (R) cleanses CC and shields — annoying in fights. His Q is shieldable. His W is a passive AS+ healing zone. No real lane threat; you outpush.

### Yuumi

Attached pet. While attached, she's untargetable to spells but you can split her off when she leaves to W or auto. Her Q (Prowling Projectile) is shieldable. Hard to kill, but does no damage on her own — focus her ADC.

### Renata Glasc

Engage-disengage support. Her W (revive) is a fight-restart problem. Her ult R is a wide AoE charm — shieldable on you. Q (root) is shieldable. Mediocre damage; you outpush.

### Morgana

Black Shield on her ADC denies your shield-eats and CC engage. Her Q (Dark Binding) is shieldable. Her R is wide AoE — Spell Shield it on cast. Push her past her low waveclear.

## Tiny

### Tahm Kench

Tongue lash (Q) is shieldable. His ult (W on ally, R global) is utility. Slow, easy to outrange. Don't stand near low minions — his Q range is deceptive.

### Taric

Pure peel. His E (stun) is shieldable. His R is invuln zone — denies your kill window but also their offense. Push hard — he has zero waveclear.

### Alistar

Combo (W+Q) is the threat — knockup + headbutt. Both are unshieldable (no projectile). His ult is mostly DR. Stay back; if he engages without ult, fight back. He's slow.

### Braum

Q (slingshot) is shieldable. His passive stun procs after 4 stacks — don't take 4 autos near him. His R is shieldable on cast. Mediocre damage on his own.

### Zilean

Roaming bomb support. His Q (bomb) is shieldable. His R is a revive — annoying. He has no waveclear and low durability. Push and deny him roams.
