# Kog'Maw — Playbook

Source: afrente do tempo's Mobafire guide (patch 26.09) + general Kog'Maw principles.

## Win conditions

Reach late game with items. Bio-Arcane Barrage (W) at full build outputs massive % max-HP damage at extreme range — you delete tanks and carries alike. Win condition is: stay alive long enough to auto-attack freely. Your support's peel and your own positioning are what win or lose games; raw damage is not the issue once you have 2+ items.

## Side selection

Group — do not split push. Kog'Maw has no mobility and no escape. You live or die based on your team's ability to peel for you. Grouping for objectives and teamfights is mandatory. Stay behind your tank/support in every skirmish.

## Early game

Farm safely. Wiggle before taking farm to avoid trading disadvantageously. Your W passive (% HP auto damage) is live at level 1 but range is short — play at max range and avoid extended trades before Guinsoo's. In 3v3 brawls, wait for enemies to blow abilities before joining. Abuse your Q armor/MR shred in short poke trades when safe.

## Mid game

If ahead: freely push lanes and look for picks — your damage is high enough to threaten anyone. If behind or even: play the same as early, wait for enemies to commit abilities, then W them down. Do not force fights. Your scaling is the win condition — a 20-minute Kog'Maw with one item is dangerous; a 30-minute Kog'Maw with three is game-ending.

## Late game

Enemy MR purchases hard-cap your damage window. Ideally win before the enemy fully completes their MR stack. Void Staff (AP path) or % HP damage from W (on-hit path) mitigate this but you cannot ignore it. Stay alive above all else — your passive (Icathian Surprise) is a free suicide bomb on death, use it as a deterrent.

## Teamfighting

Stay at maximum W range. Never step into melee range. Use E (Void Ooze) defensively — line it between you and any diver/assassin. Spam R (Living Artillery) on low-HP targets or to poke before the fight. Your job is to AA-chain the tankiest enemy or peel targets with W until they're dead. If you get dove, flash away or ghost-kite; do not fight back until you have range separation.
