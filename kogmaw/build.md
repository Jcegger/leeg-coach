# Kog'Maw — Build

Source: afrente do tempo's "26.09 BEST KOG BUILDS" (Mobafire, patch 26.09, updated May 2026). Challenger OTP, peak 1300 LP, rank 1 world twice.

## Summoner spells

- **Standard:** Barrier + Flash
- **vs CC-heavy:** Cleanse + Flash (or Cleanse + Ghost)
- **Ghost variants:** Flash + Ghost, Barrier + Ghost

## Runes

### Standard (default)
- **Precision (primary):** Press the Attack · Triumph · Legend: Alacrity · Cut Down
- **Inspiration (secondary):** Cash Back · Jack of All Trades
- **Shards:** +10% Attack Speed · +9 Adaptive · +10–180 HP

### vs Hard Engage
- **Precision (primary):** Press the Attack · Triumph · Legend: Alacrity · Cut Down
- **Resolve (secondary):** Bone Plating · Overgrowth
- **Shards:** +10% Attack Speed · +9 Adaptive · +65 Base Health

### Scaling
- **Precision (primary):** Press the Attack · Triumph · Legend: Alacrity · Cut Down
- **Sorcery (secondary):** Gathering Storm · Manaflow Band
- **Shards:** +10% Attack Speed · +9 Adaptive · +10–180 HP

## Situational item swaps

- Randuin's Omen over Runaan's Hurricane — fed crit ADC in crit core path
- Lord Dominik's Regards over Hexoptics C44 — enemy full armor stack
- Zhonya's Hourglass over Shadowflame — need survivability in AP build
- Rabadon's Deathcap as last slot — AP build, enemy has no more MR to itemize
- Wit's End over Terminus — mixed damage, need MR shred + sustain
- The Collector over Runaan's Hurricane — kill-pressure focused, squishy enemy team

## Skill order

**Standard:** W max first (Bio-Arcane Barrage — core damage + range), Q second, R at 6/11/16.
**vs Lane Bullies:** Start Q/W then E ranks 2–3 early for the slow, then W max, Q last, E finish.
