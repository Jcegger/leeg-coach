# Shen — Playbook

## Win conditions

- **Split push + global R:** take side lane, force 1-3 split by existing as a threat, then R to any teammate being dove. Shen's R is worth more than any single trade you'd win by staying.
- **Front-line engage:** E into their backline or onto their carry, W to protect your ADC during their burst window. You are the shield and the taunt — dive first, peel second.

## Side selection

Strong on both sides. Prefer the side where your AD/fed carry is alone — your R range is global. If ahead, push top side and teleport to bot dives. If behind, group mid and use R reactively.

## Early game

Shen is a short-trade champion. The combo is E → spirit blade Q for empowered AAs + magic damage. Never extended auto trade without W up — save W to block their key ability (Jax Counter Strike, Fiora riposte, Darius bleed stacks). Grasp proc on cooldown when safe to walk up.

Farm to Titanic Hydra first. You don't need to win lane — you need to not lose it and be full HP when your R is up.

## Mid game

First R usage matters. Use it when an ally is being dove and the 3s channel will actually land — don't waste it on a fight that's already decided. After ulting, assess whether to split back top or group. If you just teleported into a won fight near an objective, stay and take it.

Timing your W: activate it just before the enemy commits an ability, not after. It has a 1.5s ramp — predict the blitz punch, the Jax leap, the Draven axes.

## Late game

You are the engage and the peel simultaneously. Lead with E onto priority target, walk to your ADC, W when they focus you or your carry. Your HP pool + Thornmail + Grasp passive make you extremely hard to burst — use that to absorb cooldowns for your team.

Never R into a fight that's already lost. Use it to turn a close fight or defend a base dive — not to join a teamfight that's at 0% HP after the fight ends.

## Teamfighting

Spirit blade placement determines your value. Before the fight, position your Q blade between you and their backline so your E dash pulls it through them. E taunt → Q retrieve → W for AA block on your carry is the ideal sequence.

Prioritize taunting their fed carry or assassin. Even a 1s taunt on an Ahri or Zed prevents their full combo.
